About

Berklee College of Music student majoring in Electronic Production and Design with a double minor in Acoustics and Electronics and Creative Coding. Deep passion for acoustics, audio, guitar, production, and recording.

Current Guitar Rig

My Gibson Les Paul Axcess Custom Bengal Burst (490R/498T) feeds a BOSS TU-3 chromatic tuner, then into the Browne Protein Drive V3 (my go-to), followed by a JHS Angry Charlie V3 for high gain and an Xotic RC Booster V2 for clean or overdrive boost. Everything runs into a Fender Hot Rod Deville Red Limited Edition, with a Strymon Mobius (modulation), Timeline (delay), and Cloudburst (reverb) in the amp’s effects loop.
